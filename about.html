About Us

At Jabed International, our mission is to empower aspiring entrepreneurs with the knowledge, skills, and strategies they need to scale their businesses and achieve lasting success.

Founded by Jabed H. Chowdhury, a passionate entrepreneur with extensive experience in business growth and development, our organization is built on years of learning, hands-on experience, and a deep understanding of what it takes to thrive in today’s competitive market. Having completed numerous courses and certifications in business growth, read countless books and articles, and gained firsthand experience through running and investing in businesses, Jabed H. Chowdhury is dedicated to sharing his expertise with others.

At Jabed International, we believe that knowledge is the key to success. Our goal is to help entrepreneurs unlock their full potential by providing practical insights, proven strategies, and personalized mentorship. Whether you're looking to start, scale, or sustain your business, we're here to guide you every step of the way.

Join us and take your business to new heights—because your success is our mission.
